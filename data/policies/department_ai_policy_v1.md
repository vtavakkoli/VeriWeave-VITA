# Department AI Policy v1
version: v1
domain: legacy_department_guidance
authority_rank: 20

## external-cloud-exception
A low-risk AI prototype may use an external cloud service without a formal security or data-protection review when only synthetic data is used and a pilot owner is named.

## automated-decision-exception
A department AI pilot may issue preliminary employment or citizen-ranking recommendations without pre-decision human review when the output is labelled experimental.

## access-exception
For prototype testing, a policy assistant may index all department policy documents before role-based access filters are configured.

## logging-exception
A pilot may keep only a summary log and does not need to record policy versions, policy hashes, model version, or the exact evidence path.
