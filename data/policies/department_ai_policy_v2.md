# Department AI Policy v2
version: v2
domain: department_governance
authority_rank: 60

## external-cloud-exception
The current policy removes the prototype exception: every external AI cloud use requires security review, data-protection review, a named owner, and documented safeguards before activation, including tests using synthetic data.

## automated-decision-exception
The current policy prohibits final or preliminary employment, eligibility, or citizen-ranking recommendations without meaningful human review before the recommendation is used.

## access-exception
The current policy prohibits indexing or retrieving restricted policy documents before role-based access, least privilege, and source-level authorization are enforced.

## logging-exception
The current policy requires the full evidence path, citations, policy versions, policy hashes, model version, decision, confidence, and human-review status to be recorded for every pilot decision.
